Software Debugging Event Details

Event Rules & Regulations

Mail for submission as per event rules: elite2k24@kanchiuniv.ac.in

  1. One participant per team.
  2. Certificates will be provided for all participants.
  3. First and second prizes are awarded based on judgment.
  4. Students have to maintain a decent dress code.

Rounds

First Round:

  1. This round consists of 20 questions
  2. Time duration: 20 minutes
  3. Maximum numbers of outputs should be given, and the respective logics must be answered
  4. The best performances are promoted for GRAND FINALE

Final Round:

  1. 5 programs are given to individuals on the spot
  2. Time duration: 45 minutes
  3. The participants who debugged the maximum number of programs will be considered as best performer
  4. Best performances are rewarded
